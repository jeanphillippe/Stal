How it works

From search to stall, in minutes

Stalbox connects horse owners with establishments directly. We handle discovery and trust — you handle the rest.

01

Search on the map

Filter by location and radius, or select a province. Browse pins for every active location — click any to preview.

02

Register & subscribe

Create your account and subscribe for €3/month. Unlock prices, contact details, and booking requests.

03

Send a request

Message the establishment with your horses, dates, and questions. They confirm directly — no platform cut.

04

Add to calendar

Once your booking is approved, both parties get a one-click "Add to Google Calendar" button.
